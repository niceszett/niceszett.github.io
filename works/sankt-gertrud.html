Brutalism at Sankt Gertrud: church+culture

Based on the vision of the Cologne music label 'Brutalism', which explores the interface between architecture and sound, a concert will take place in St. Gertrud church (by Gottfried Bhm) on 29 October 2022. The focus is on the idea of sound-based brutalism, meaning the attempt to transfer the understanding of materiality and texture prevalent in brutalism to a sonic level. Several artists will be invited to perform concerts in St. Gertrud throughout the evening.

The concept provides the framework for an artistic exploration of the theme on a spatial-visual level and is the starting point for the development of a work that will be exhibited in the church alongside the concerts. Besides, it has led to the development of my own software platform that aims to create and control generative images in real-time by making use of a modular approach that resembles the workflow found in audio synthesis.
