Earthseeds

The world surrounding us can only be experienced by us to a certain extent, because the smallest of all life often remains hidden from our sensory perception. Nevertheless, we are able to direct our attention to these specific forms and to make visible what would otherwise stay invisible through the aid of technology.

In this context, 'Earthseeds' deals with the ecology of the forest and its inhabitants by making use of macro lenses.
