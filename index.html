Space-time Simulation

What happens when the human body, in terms of its anatomy, becomes part of a self-contained system that can be influenced by motion? 

'Space-time Simulation' takes up the phenomenon of analogue video feedback, which was extensively studied in 1984 by the physicist James P. Crutchfield for its usability as an experimental system for investigating complex spatial and temporal dynamics.
