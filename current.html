- St. Gertrud church, Cologne

Brutalism at sankt gertrud: church+culture

Based on the vision of the Cologne music label 'Brutalism', which explores the interface between architecture and sound, a concert will take place in St. Gertrud church (by Gottfried Böhm) on 29 October 2022. The focus is on the idea of sound-based brutalism, meaning the attempt to transfer the understanding of materiality and texture prevalent in brutalism to a sonic level. Several artists will be invited to perform concerts in St. Gertrud throughout the evening.

The concept provides the framework for an artistic exploration of the theme on a spatial-visual level and is the starting point for the development of a work that will be exhibited in the church alongside the concerts.
